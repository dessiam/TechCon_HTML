Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers passionate about innovation. Over the years, it has grown into one of the world's leading technology conferences, attracting thousands of engineers, entrepreneurs, and industry leaders.

The conference has expanded internationally, hosting events across Europe, North America, and Africa. Today, TechCon continues to empower the global tech community by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and groundbreaking announcements.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to advancing the future of technology by bringing together diverse voices, bold ideas, and world-changing innovations. We aim to inspire attendees through visionary talks, hands-on workshops, and countless opportunities for collaboration.

Whether you're a beginner or an industry expert, our mission is to support your growth and empower you to create impactful solutions that shape tomorrow.
